### ⭐ The 60-second version
- **Stories convert, not reels/posts** — buy 2-week story rights, run on Meta, scale-or-kill.
- **"Saturation is king"** — brand a proven, unbranded product and become "the original."
- **~20 creators drive ~80%** of revenue — found by testing ~200; then obsess over the winners.
- **Judge creators on conversion, not followers** — performance media buying with creators as the creative source.
- **Decide fast, let the customer (not your taste) be the judge.**
